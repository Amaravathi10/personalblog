Science

B.M. Birla
Science Center

Khairatabad-Hyd

Birla Planetarium Hyderabad: A Celestial Experience
My visit to the Birla Planetarium Hyderabad was nothing short of extraordinary. It's a place where science meets art, where wonder meets understanding, and where the mysteries of the universe are brought to life. Whether you're a seasoned astronomer or simply someone with a curiosity about the cosmos, a visit to the Birla Planetarium is an experience that will leave you inspired, enlightened, and in awe of the beauty of the universe.
